Enjoy creating and reading poems.

Simplicity without compromising.

Features

Easily create a new poem in four easy steps. Enter the poem title. Then write your masterpiece in the body. Pick or create a new theme(s) for your masterpiece and a category and you're all done.
Now other poetry lovers and readers can be able to enjoy your piece and you can get to revel in the new found hobby and the creativity you have.

Do you have a poem saved as a file on your device? There's no need to write it again or have to do a copy and paste. You can just load the file and the poem title and body will be automatically read and filled in. The file name without the extension will be the poem title.

Is it the happy themed or the romantic poems you want to read? You can just filter by the themes you want and poems having that theme will be shown. If multiple themes are selected, then poems having all those themes are displayed.
This feature works on poems that are currently being displayed therefore, when applied after a search or when in a particular category, then it'll only be applied on the results of the search or the poems in that category.

About

The love for poetry and other creative writings motivated us to do this project. Everyone, in their own capacity, think different from the other and from those thoughts, each one can craft their own special pieces of poetry. This project seeks to give everyone a chance to write and share their pieces in as simple manner as possible while providing great user experience.

This project was started as a portfolio project for the Software Engineering course at Holberton School.
